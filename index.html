About the Event

A Celebration of Nature

Prithivi Pulse is an annual inter-event initiative by the Environment Club of K.R. Mangalam University, dedicated to fostering environmental awareness and creative expression among students.

We believe the pulse of the Earth — Prithivi — beats through every one of us. This event is a call to listen, to act, and to express that connection through intellect, art, and photography.

Sub Events

Three Ways to Participate

Choose your arena of expression. All competitions are open to individual participants.

🎙️

Green Vichaar Sabha: A debate competition

Voice your perspective on critical environmental topics. Defend or challenge — let your arguments echo the need for change.

  • 📌 Individual participation
  • 📌 Choose your stance: For or Against the topic
  • 📌 Topic will be shared on the day of the event
👗

Nature on the Ramp: Sustainable Eco-Fashion Ramp Walk

Showcase sustainable fashion designs or eco-friendly attire on the ramp. Walk for nature, walk for the future.

  • 📌 Individual participation
  • 📌 Sustainable fashion designs or eco-friendly attire
  • 📌 Original creations preferred
🖼️

Green Canvas: a poster making competition

Design a compelling poster that communicates an environmental message. Art has the power to move hearts and change minds.

  • 📌 Solo or Duo participation
  • 📌 Theme: Environment & Sustainability
  • 📌 Materials will be provided on-site
