About the Event

A Celebration of Nature

Prithivi Pulse 1.0 is an annual interUniversity-event initiative by the Environment Club of K.R. Mangalam University, dedicated to fostering environmental awareness and creative expression among individual, societies and communities.

We believe the pulse of the Earth — Prithivi — beats through every one of us. This event is a call to listen, to act and to express that connection through intellect, art and live.

Events

Three Ways to Participate

Choose your arena of expression. All competitions are open to all Colleges & Universities.

🎙️

Green Vichaar Sabha: An Elocution competition

Voice your perspective on critical environmental topics related to planet Earth. Defend or challenge — let your arguments echo the need for change.

  • 📌 Individual participation
  • 📌 Choose your Topics from the Brochure
👗

Nature on the Ramp: Sustainable Eco-Fashion Ramp Walk

Showcase sustainable fashion designs or eco-friendly attire on the ramp. Walk for nature, walk for the future.

  • 📌 Team participation
  • 📌 Costumes should involve eco-friendly, recycled or upcycled materials or messages.
  • 📌 Original creations preferred
🖼️

Green Canvas: A poster making competition

Art has the power to move hearts and change minds. Design a compelling poster that communicates about the Earth.

  • 📌 Individual participation
  • 📌 Theme: Related to Earth
  • 📌 Drawing Sheets will be provided at the Venue
